First I wanted to be

Veterinarians treat disease, disorder or injury in animals,
which includes diagnosis, treatment and aftercare.
The scope of practice, specialty and experience of the
individual veterinarian will dictate exactly what interventions
they perform, but most will perform surgery
(of differing complexity).

As a veterinarian and
lover of animals.

Veterinarians typically do the following:
Examine animals to assess their health and diagnose problems. Treat and dress wounds. Perform surgery on animals. Test for and vaccinate against diseases. Operate medical equipment, such as x-ray machines.
